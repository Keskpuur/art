Jana Mätas

------------

“OLI SIIS ÜKSKORD NAGU SIIN. VÕI SIIS EI OLE”

gravel roads from all directions led to our country home. The cars always drove so that there was a dust cloud behind them. All the plants along the road were covered in a thick layer of dust.

And then I. Barefoot on the gravel road. Dust between my teeth and gray legs up to my knees. You had to step very gently so that your soles wouldn't hurt too much. Sometimes you could find some that sparkled among the dusty stones."
